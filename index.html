priceera is a new CPQ pricing tool specifically tailored to the process industry, where complex pricing models are the norm.
Unlike most pricing solutions that predominantly serve discrete manufacturers and retailers, priceera excels in dynamic, formula-driven pricing, making it ideal for more challenging industries that deal in continuous and semi-continuous processes with highly customized products.
Our solution significantly reduces pricing and quoting time while minimizing errors, offering a streamlined and efficient experience. Designed to be cost-effective, priceera minimizes the need for extensive customization, making it a smart choice for SMEs and large enterprises seeking a robust pricing tool without the substantial investment typically required by traditional CPQ CRM systems.
